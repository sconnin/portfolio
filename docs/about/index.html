About

Here are a few odds and ends to get the ball rolling:

A microscope and dissecting kit were my favorite Christmas gifts as a kid. I can and have built homes from foundation to roof. I wore overalls on the first date with my soon-to-become wife. She hasn’t forgotten.

I also have an affinity for statistical models & data viz, expanding educational access, teamwork, strong coffee, Gibson archtops, and trilobites.

With a PhD in earth and environmental sciences and 20+ years of professional experience, I have started and led award winning programs, published peer reviewed research, and worked to expand educational opportunities for adult learners. I still count myself among the latter.

Currently, I am working as an independent consultant and remain open to new challenges and opportunities.
